Explanatory text property sheet

Enter any line of explanatory text in the text entry field.

Specify the position of the text item

  • in terms of its horizontal distance from the preceding form element (field or explanatory text) on the same level of nesting ("horizontal space"),

  • or as a tab position (in character spaces) relative to the left margin of the containing form or sub-form box ("horizontal tab"),

  • and/or by the number of line breaks to be inserted before the text ("vertical space").
