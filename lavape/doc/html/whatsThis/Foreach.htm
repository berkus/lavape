Foreach

Use foreach to search a finite set of Lava objects for objects that fulfill a certain condition (expressed by the where clause) and optionally do certain things with these (in the do clause).

The where clause may be omitted. The do clause is mandatory then.

The do clause may be omitted. The where clause is mandatory then (and "where" is replaced with "holds true:" then).
