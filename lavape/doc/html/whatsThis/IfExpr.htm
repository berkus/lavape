If-then-elsif-else expression

The Lava if-then-elsif-else conditional expression is different from corresponding statements in other languages in so far as its if- and elsif-conditions aren't expressions but statements and as such may be true or false. This is a consequence of the logical semantics of Lava.

The elsif (but not the else) branches are optional and may be omitted (by deleting them using the DEL key or the scissors button).
