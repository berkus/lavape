"Old" expression

The old expression may be used in the bodies of functions and postconditions. It is evaluated on entry to the body of the underlying function. The resulting value is stored and delivered when the old expression is encountered during the actual execution of the function or of an attached postcondition. It is used primarily in postconditions to specify the intended modification of a variable or expression by the function to which the postcondition belongs.
