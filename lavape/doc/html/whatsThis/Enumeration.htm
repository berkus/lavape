Enumeration

All enumeration types are derived from the common base type "Enumeration" in std.lava (see there!).

They are basically ordered lists of enumerated items that are designated by identifiers. The item identifiers must be unique within their containing enumerated type. Two enumerated types may use identical identifiers since Lava generates unique internal identifiers anyway, but you should avoid such name clashes for readability reasons. In case of doubt use the "Go to declaration" button to find out where the item has been declared.
