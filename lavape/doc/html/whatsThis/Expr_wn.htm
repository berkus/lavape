Current selection: <expr>

Your next editing step may be one of the following:

  • Insert an executable Lava expression for <expr> by clicking one of the enabled expression buttons
    • on the keyword toolbar (left side of the LavaPE main window) or
    • on the operations toolbar (containing particularly the various arithmetic and bitwise logical operations).
  • If the selected <expr> placeholder is already an operand of an operation with possibly multiple operands (like + or *) then you may press the Return key or click the "Insert" button to insert another <expr> placeholder after the current selection (or before, if the "Insert before" button is in "on" state).
  • Click once more on the selected <expr> to open a little edit window where you may insert a constant. Alternatively you may click the spectacles or press the "Cursor down" key to open the <expr> for editing. Press the "Return" key to commit your data entry; alternatively you may press the "Cursor up" key. Click anywhere outside the little edit window to cancel the edit operation without changing the <expr>.

Note: In the first case a nested expression is inserted (in most cases enclosed in parentheses) in place of the original <expr> placeholder, whereas in the second case just another operand is added without parentheses.
