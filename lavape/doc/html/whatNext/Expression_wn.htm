Current selection: A constant or other Lava expression.

Your next edit step may be one of the following:

  • Insert another executable Lava expression for the selected expression by clicking one of the enabled expression buttons
    • on the keyword toolbar (left side of the LavaPE main window) or
    • on the operations toolbar (containing particularly the various arithmetic and bitwise logical operations).
  • If the selected expression is already an operand of an operation with possibly multiple operands (like + or *) then you may press the Return key or click the "Insert" button to insert another <expr> placeholder after the current selection (or before, if the "Insert before" button  is in "on" state).
  • If the selected expression is just a constant: Press the "cursor down" arrow key or click once more on the variable name or click the spectacles button to open a little edit window in which you can edit the constant then. Thereafter press the "return" key or the "cursor up" key to close the little edit window.

Note: In the first case, if you click the tool button of an operation with possibly multiple operands then your originally selected expression will become the first operand of the inserted new operation (or the second, if the "Insert before" button  is in "on" state). The new operation will be enclosed in parentheses in most cases.
