Current selection: A <varName> placeholder in the declaration of a local variable.

Your next edit step may  be one of the following:

  • Press the "cursor down" arrow key or click once more on the <varName> placeholder or click the spectacles button to open a little edit window in which you can enter a new variable name then. After you have defined the new variable name in this way you may press the "return" key or the "cursor up" key to close the little edit window.
  • Press the Return key or click the "Insert" button to insert another <varName> placeholder after the current selection (or before, if the "Insert before" button  is in "on" state).
